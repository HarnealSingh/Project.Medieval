MEDICINE!!!

Not many people knew what had caused such illness's to occur, the Catholic Church said that it was because of sinful behaviour. The medicine used to treat diseases and sicknesse's were made from herbs, spices, and resins, these medicines were applied in many forms such as baths, drinks, pills, washes, rubs, poultices, purges and ointments. For the Black Death however, only 30-40% of people would survive so the doctors decided to apply a warm poultice of butter, onion, and garlic on the buboes that appeared on the body.
